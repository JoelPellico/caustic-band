WHO WE ARE:

CAUSTIC

In the year 2019 the names of those who would be known as "The Caustic" began to be heard. The early band consisted of Frank Fire, the drummer and their early pioneers, Leonard and Rommie. Finally it is completed with the arrival of Shohn Skull on guitar and Joel Wildhunter on bass. This would cause an explosion in the Argentine heavy metal night! We released "Arrowfire", a demo that came in time of pandemic and was a success even internationally. Caustic’s sound would be known from Argentina to Brazil, Peru, Spain and the Czech Republic.
In 2022 Caustic welcomes its new vocalist, Nick Disaster. With him would begin this new musical stage touring bars, theaters, giving shows. Where Caustic arrived, the party began! And so it will remain!
